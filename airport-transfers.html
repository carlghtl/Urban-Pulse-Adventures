About Our Airport Transfer Service

Travel from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport to your hotel or accommodation with confidence. UrbanPulse airport transfers are built for guests who value punctuality, comfort, and reliable arrival support.

Whether you arrive alone, with family, or in a group, our services provide clear communication, helpful drivers, and safe vehicles. We monitor flight status so you can land without worrying about delays or pickup coordination.

Arrival support: You get airport meet & greet, baggage assistance, direction to your hotel, and advice on local routes. Our drivers know Nairobi's major hotel neighborhoods, including Westlands, Upper Hill, Karen, and the central business district.

What's Included

  • Professional certified driver
  • Air-conditioned comfortable vehicle
  • Direct airport to hotel transfer
  • Flight tracking and real-time updates
  • All tolls and parking included

Not Included

  • Stops beyond your final destination
  • Driver waiting time (available separately)
  • Personal expenses outside service scope

Booking Information

Reserve your transfer at least 2–3 hours before arrival. Provide your flight details, arrival time, and hotel address so we can coordinate your pickup accurately.

Pickup point: Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) arrivals.

Coverage area: All major hotels and accommodations in Nairobi, including Westlands, Upper Hill, Karen, Langata, and central Nairobi; international hotel brands, boutique stays, and serviced apartments; guesthouses, Airbnb properties, and private residences; extended transfers to Nairobi National Park and nearby destinations.

Special requests: Contact us for group transfers or special service requests.

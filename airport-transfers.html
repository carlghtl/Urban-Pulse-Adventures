About Our Airport Transfer Service

Experience stress-free airport transfers from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport (JKIA) to your hotel or accommodation across Nairobi and surrounding areas. Our professional drivers and modern fleet ensure comfortable, safe, and reliable transfers every time.

Whether you're arriving alone, with family, or as a group, we have the perfect transfer option for you. Book in advance for guaranteed availability and enjoy competitive pricing. Our drivers are courteous, knowledgeable about Nairobi, and committed to making your journey smooth and enjoyable.

Service Details: We offer 24/7 airport transfer services with real-time flight tracking. Meet your driver at the arrivals hall with a personalized name sign (on Premium and Luxury packages). All vehicles are regularly maintained and insured for your safety and peace of mind.

What's Included in All Transfers

  • Professional certified driver
  • Air-conditioned comfortable vehicle
  • Direct airport to hotel transfer
  • Flight tracking and real-time updates
  • All tolls and parking included

Not Included

  • Stops beyond your final destination
  • Driver waiting time (available separately)
  • Personal expenses outside service scope

Coverage Area & Hotels

We provide transfers to all major hotels and accommodations in Nairobi including:

  • Hotels in Westlands, Upper Hill, and Karen
  • Serena, Hilton, and Safari Park Hotel
  • Boutique hotels and serviced apartments
  • Airbnb properties and private residences
  • Extended transfers to Nairobi National Park, Ent Park, and surrounding areas

Booking Requirements: Please book at least 2-3 hours before your flight arrival. Provide your flight details, arrival time, and hotel address during booking. Contact us if you need to reschedule or have special requests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our tours and services

You can book a tour through our website by clicking the "Book" button on any tour page, or by contacting us directly via email at urbanpulseadventures254.org@gmail.com. We'll respond within 24 hours with confirmation details.

Each tour description clearly outlines what's included. Generally, our prices include:

  • Professional local guide
  • Guided tour experience
  • Community interaction fees
  • Basic refreshments

Exclusions may include transport to/from the meeting point, meals, and personal expenses.

We recommend bringing:

  • Comfortable walking shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Camera (where permitted)
  • Sun protection (hat, sunscreen, sunglasses)
  • Small amount of local currency
  • Any personal medications

Our cancellation policy varies by tour. Generally:

  • More than 7 days before: Full refund
  • 3-7 days before: 50% refund
  • Less than 3 days: No refund

For specific tour policies, please check the tour description or contact us.

Most of our tours are family-friendly. However, some tours may have age restrictions due to content or physical requirements. Please check the specific tour details or contact us to discuss suitability for children.

Our primary language is English. However, many of our guides speak additional languages including Swahili and various local dialects. If you have specific language requirements, please let us know when booking.

Yes! We offer customized tours to meet your specific interests and requirements. Contact us with your ideas and we'll work with you to create a personalized experience.

We accept various payment methods including bank transfer, mobile money (M-Pesa), and major credit cards. Payment details will be provided upon booking confirmation.

Yes, safety is our priority. All our tours:

  • Are coordinated with community leaders
  • Are led by trained, certified local guides
  • Follow established safety protocols
  • Include pre-tour safety briefings

We also provide comprehensive safety guidelines to all travelers.

Photography is generally allowed, but we have strict guidelines:

  • Always ask permission before photographing individuals
  • Respect when someone declines
  • Some areas may have photography restrictions
  • Commercial use requires separate consent

Your guide will provide specific guidance for each location.

In case of adverse weather conditions, we will:

  • Contact you as early as possible
  • Offer to reschedule to a convenient date
  • Provide a full refund if rescheduling isn't possible

Your safety is our priority, and we may need to modify or cancel tours accordingly.

We recommend booking:

  • Standard tours: At least 2-3 days in advance
  • Popular tours (weekends): 1 week in advance
  • Custom/specialized tours: 2 weeks in advance

Last-minute bookings may be possible depending on availability.

Yes, we offer discounts for groups of 4 or more. Contact us with your group size and preferred dates, and we'll provide a customized quote.

We strive to accommodate special requirements. Please contact us before booking to discuss your needs. We'll work to find or create a suitable tour option for you.

We value your feedback! After your tour, you'll receive an email with a feedback form. You can also email us directly at urbanpulseadventures254.org@gmail.com with any comments, suggestions, or concerns.

Still have questions?

Contact us and we'll be happy to help!

Travel Insurance

Protect your trip and travel with peace of mind

Why Travel Insurance?

While Urban Pulse Adventures takes every precaution to ensure safe and enjoyable experiences, travel insurance provides essential protection against unforeseen circumstances that can disrupt or interrupt your trip.

Recommended Coverage

We strongly recommend ensuring your travel insurance includes:

Medical Coverage

  • Emergency Medical Expenses: Coverage for hospital visits, medical treatment, and evacuation
  • Emergency Evacuation: Coverage for medical evacuation to your home country or nearest adequate medical facility
  • Repatriation: Coverage for returning home in case of serious illness or injury
  • Minimum Recommended: $100,000 USD for medical expenses

Trip Protection

  • Trip Cancellation: Reimbursement if you need to cancel due to illness, injury, or other covered reasons
  • Trip Interruption: Coverage if you need to cut your trip short
  • Trip Delay: Reimbursement for additional expenses due to delays
  • Missed Connection: Coverage for missed departures due to circumstances beyond your control

Personal Coverage

  • Personal Liability: Protection if you're held responsible for injury or property damage
  • Personal Belongings: Coverage for lost, stolen, or damaged luggage and possessions
  • Travel Documents: Coverage for replacement of lost passport or travel documents
  • Cash and Banking: Coverage for unauthorized use of credit cards or lost cash

Specific Considerations for Kenya

  • Malaria Prevention: Some insurance policies may require proof of malaria prophylaxis
  • Yellow Fever: If traveling from or through yellow fever zones, ensure vaccination coverage
  • Adventure Activities: Confirm coverage if your tour includes any adventure elements
  • High Altitude: If visiting high-altitude areas, verify altitude-related coverage

What to Look For in a Policy

  • 24/7 emergency assistance hotline
  • Direct payment to hospitals (where possible)
  • Coverage for the specific regions you'll visit in Kenya
  • No or low deductible options
  • COVID-19 related coverage (if applicable)
  • Coverage for pre-existing medical conditions

Before You Book

  1. Review Your Existing Coverage: Check if you have any existing travel insurance through credit cards or other policies
  2. Compare Policies: Get quotes from multiple providers to compare coverage and prices
  3. Read the Fine Print: Understand exclusions, limitations, and claim procedures
  4. Keep Documentation: Save all policy documents and emergency contact numbers
  5. Share Itinerary: Provide your travel insurance details to a trusted contact

Emergency Contacts

In case of emergency during your trip, contact:

  • Your Insurance Provider: The 24/7 emergency number on your policy
  • Urban Pulse: urbanpulseadventures254.org@gmail.com
  • Local Emergency: 999 (Police), 999 (Ambulance)
  • Your Country's Embassy: Contact information for your country's embassy in Kenya

Claim Process

If you need to make a claim:

  1. Contact your insurance provider immediately
  2. Document everything (receipts, photos, medical reports)
  3. Keep copies of all correspondence
  4. Obtain police report if applicable
  5. Submit all documentation within policy timeframes

Note

Urban Pulse Adventures is not an insurance provider and cannot recommend specific policies. We encourage you to research and select a policy that meets your individual needs. Travel insurance is an additional cost, but it provides invaluable peace of mind when exploring new destinations.

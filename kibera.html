About This Tour

Experience Kibera like never before — a carefully guided journey into one of Nairobi's most dynamic communities. Our Kibera Street Vibes tour blends community-led experiences, street food, vibrant murals, and hands-on cultural moments to give you an authentic and respectful view of local life.

Join local guides and community partners as we explore markets, creative spaces, and social enterprises. Expect meaningful interactions, photography opportunities, and a deepened understanding of the neighborhood.

Itinerary (sample): The day begins at 09:00 with a warm welcome and safety briefing at the Kibera Community Hub. From there we'll walk through curated routes that highlight the area's art, food, markets, and social projects.

What's Included

  • Guided neighborhood walk and community interactions
  • Local guide and community hosts
  • Lunch at a local eatery
  • Photography tips and guided photo-walk

Not Included

  • Personal expenses and extra purchases
  • Tips and gratuities
  • Travel insurance

Meeting Point

Kibera Community Hub — Main Entrance

Important Information

  • Comfortable walking shoes recommended; moderate walking involved.
  • Respect local customs and ask before photographing people up-close.
  • Children are welcome on certain packages—check age guidance for each package.
  • Tour operates in all weather conditions; please dress appropriately.

5-Day Safari Itinerary

A carefully crafted journey through Kenya's premier wildlife destinations

1

Nairobi to Masai Mara

Depart Nairobi early morning for Masai Mara Reserve. Arrive at your luxury lodge for lunch, then head out for your first afternoon game drive. Watch the African sunset over the savanna as wildlife becomes active.

Afternoon Drive Luxury Lodge
2

Full Day Masai Mara

Early morning game drive to catch predators in action. Visit the Mara River to see hippos and crocodiles. Afternoon at leisure by the pool or spa. Evening game drive with spotlight for nocturnal wildlife.

Full Day Safari Luxury Lodge
3

Masai Mara to Amboseli

Morning game drive as the sun rises over the plains. After breakfast, depart for Amboseli National Park — the "Land of the Giants." Arrive in the afternoon for an evening game drive with Mount Kilimanjaro views.

Morning & Evening Drive Kilimanjaro Lodge
4

Full Day Amboseli

Dawn game drive to capture elephants with Kilimanjaro backdrop. Visit Maasai village for cultural immersion. Afternoon game drive to explore the swamps and wetlands — a bird watcher's paradise with 400+ species.

Full Day Safari Kilimanjaro Lodge
5

Amboseli to Nairobi

Final morning game drive with sunrise over Mount Kilimanjaro. Return to lodge for breakfast, then depart for Nairobi. Drop-off at your hotel or the airport, concluding an unforgettable safari adventure.

Morning Drive Drop-off: Nairobi

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your safari

The best time for a Kenyan safari is during the dry seasons: January to March and June to October. June to October offers the best wildlife viewing as animals gather around water sources. The Great Migration in Masai Mara typically occurs from July to October.

Essential packing list: neutral-colored clothing, comfortable walking shoes, sun hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, insect repellent, binoculars, camera with zoom lens, power adapter, and any personal medications. We provide a detailed packing list upon booking.

Yes, children aged 6 and above are welcome on our safaris. We recommend the Premium Safari package for families as it offers more flexibility and comfortable accommodations. We can arrange child-friendly activities and private vehicles for families.

Free cancellation up to 30 days before departure. 50% refund for cancellations 15-29 days before. No refund for cancellations within 14 days. We strongly recommend purchasing travel insurance for unexpected changes.

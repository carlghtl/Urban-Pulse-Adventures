About This Tour

Experience Nairobi like never before — an after-dark journey through pulsating clubs, rooftop sunsets, intimate street food moments, and serene stargazing. This carefully curated evening blends culture, music, cuisine and local stories into one memorable night.

Whether you're a party enthusiast or a culture seeker, this tour offers unique urban experiences guided by local storytellers and experts.

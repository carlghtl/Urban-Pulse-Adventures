About This Experience

Welcome to Lens & Legends — Nairobi's premier photography tour experience! Whether you're a beginner with a smartphone or a professional with DSLR gear, our expert local photographers will guide you through the city's most photogenic spots.

Discover hidden alleys, vibrant markets, stunning architecture, and the everyday beauty of Nairobi's urban landscape. Our guides are not just photographers — they're storytellers who know the city's best-kept secrets and can help you capture moments that tell a story.

What makes us different? We don't just take you to tourist spots. We show you the real Nairobi — from the golden hour light on bustling streets to the quiet corners where local life unfolds. You'll learn composition techniques, lighting tips, and how to capture authentic moments.
